Really Cool Facts about the Internet that will Blow Your Mind

You use the internet everyday but I bet you don't know half of these really cool facts.

1. Over 3.2 billion people use the internet. That's almost half of the world's population!

2. ” Gangnam Style ” by PSY is the most viewed video of all time. It has over 3,251,539,264 views!

3. About 1/3 of Italians have never used the internet! They must be too busy eating.

4. About 269 billion emails are sent per day! Imagine that!

5. There are treatment camps in China for people with internet addictions. There is some debate going on about whether or not to classify internet addiction as a mental disorder.
6. 72 hours of video content is uploaded to YouTube in just one minute! Now that's a lot of content!
