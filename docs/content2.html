Who Invented the Internet?

Some awesome people made the internet possible. Read about some of them here!

1. Leonard Kleinrock

Leonard Kleinrock, a distinguished professor of computer science at UCLA, was essential to the creation of the internet. Kleinrock developed the mathematical theory of packet switching. He also was a great influence to ARPAnet.

2. J. C. R. Licklider

Licklider, a psychologist and computer scientist, created an intergalactic network concept. He is called the Johnny Appleseed of computing for laying the foundations of computing.

3. Robert Kahn

Robert Kahn is an electrical engineer who co-created TCP and IP, the communication protocols used by the Internet.

4. Tim Berners-Lee

Tim Berners-Lee, an engineer and computer scientist, is credited with the invention of the World Wide Web(WWW). He is also the director for the World Wide Web Consortium, which is responsible for the continual development of the web.

5. Vint Cerf

Vint Cerf is often called one of "the father's of the internet" for his co-creation of TCP and IP. He was a manager for DARPA and was a key player in the formation of ICANN.
