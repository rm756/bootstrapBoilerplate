History of the Internet Timeline

The story of how the internet became what it is today.

1957

Sputnik I is launched

USSR successfully launch Sputnik, the first artificial satellite into space

1958

ARPA is created

Eisenhower creates the Advanced Research Project Agency(ARPA) to improve the technology for the U.S. military.

1961

Packet Switching

Kleinrock writes “ Information Flow in Large Communication Nets”, which introduces the idea of packet switching

1972

ARPAnet Introduced to Public

Robert Kahn introduces ARPAnet to the public at the International Computer Communication Conference. Kahn does this by connecting 20 computers.

1991

”Information Superhighway”

The High-performance Computing and communications act of 1991 is created by Al Gore. This act creates the National Research an Education Network and the National Information Infrastructure, also known as the Information Superhighway.

1991

WWW Made Public

The World Wide Web opens to the public. This made sharing information and communicating much easier.
